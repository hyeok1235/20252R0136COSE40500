# Venture Capitalist & Start-up

**Topic:** Investment, Entrepreneurship

## 1. Venture Capital (VC)

### Key Success Factors

- **Domain expertise:** Enter with knowledge of your field
- **Network:** Critical importance of connections
  - Must connect with entrepreneurs, individual investors, and investment banks
- **Trust:** Essential due to high-risk, high-return nature

### Key Metrics

- **ROI (Return on Investment):** Basic return measure
- **IRR (Internal Rate of Return):** More important for PE/VC
  - Incorporates time as a variable
  - Reflects the efficiency of capital deployment over time

## 2. Start-up Investment Cases

### APR (Success Case)
- Company behind Medicube and other brands
- Surpassed Amorepacific (5 trillion KRW) to reach 10 trillion KRW valuation
- Exit at 250 billion KRW

### BRINICLE (Dontok)
- Messenger service launched during the "messenger wars" era
- Competed in highly saturated market

## Key Takeaways

- VC success depends heavily on network and domain knowledge
- IRR matters more than simple ROI in venture investing
- Timing and market positioning are crucial for startups
